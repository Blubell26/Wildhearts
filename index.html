Inside the guide

Four weeks. Four shifts.

Each week builds on the last. Each one goes somewhere the previous one couldn't.

Week One

Waking Up

Before anything can change, you have to be willing to see where you actually are. Not the version you present to the world but the real one. We start here.

Week Two

Desire & Fear

There are two kinds of desire and two kinds of fear. Most of us have never been taught to tell them apart. That changes this week.

Week Three

Choosing Differently

You don't just make choices. Over time, your choices make you. This week is about seeing the patterns and making one quiet decision to begin changing them.

Week Four

Building Your Life

The path doesn't appear before you step onto it. It appears as you walk. This week is about taking the next honest step without waiting until you're sure.

Every week includes a core teaching, daily practices, and reflection prompts to work with throughout your days. A complete printable workbook is included — yours to keep.
